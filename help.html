Help & Support

How do I upload files?

Click on the "Upload" button in the dashboard or file sections, then select your files to upload.

Can I organize my files?

Yes! Use tags and folders to categorize and organize your uploads efficiently.

How is billing calculated?

Billing is based on the total storage space used. You pay only for what you use.

How do I share files?

You can share files by clicking the share icon (ðŸ”—) next to your uploaded items.

Can I restore deleted files?

Yes, deleted files can be restored from the Trash folder within 30 days of deletion.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Account > Change Password. Follow the on-screen instructions to update your credentials.

How can I cancel or downgrade my plan?

Navigate to the "My Plan" page and choose the option to cancel or downgrade your subscription.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, PayStore is available for both Android and iOS platforms. Search for "PayStore" on your app store.
